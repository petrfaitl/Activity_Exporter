Activity Exporter

Utility to convert sportswatch workout files to gpx format

Hang on release coming soon

About this project

This project had started as a response a Christmas 2014 outage of the Movescount website. This is a Suunto owned and operated site that is used to support Suunto watches and devices.

YOUR SPORTS DIARY

Store every single workout you do with the amount of detail you want. Whether you are a competing athlete, an outdoor maniac, a technical diver or just getting started with your new active life - Movescount is for you!

As you require an active connection to this website to export your workout logs from your watch, it became necessary to design a tool to analyse and convert Suunto workout files to a standard recognised format, such as .gpx. These can be used at other online analytics services, for example Strava.
